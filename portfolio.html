Exploring the Relationship between Education, Marital Status, and Salary
I explored the effect of education level and marital status on whether a person's salary was greater than $50k USD annually (1994, worldwide) using RStudio data science functions and libraries.

Pokémon #0001-0500 Exploratory Analysis
I created a dataset of all Pokémon with Pokédex ID #0001-0500 detailing the Pokémon's primary type, height, weight, HP, attack, defense, and more using PokéAPI in Python (https://pokeapi.co) in order to discover the relationship between a Pokemon's physical attributes (height, weight, etc.), battle attributes (HP, attack, defense, etc.), and primary typing. I analyzed and modeled data as a Pandas dataframe in Jupyter Notebook.

Exploring Schizophrenia-Related Suicide
